Made by Justin Quillinan in about a day. Tweet me why don't you.

A visualisation for Culture Hack Scotland based on the 2010 Edinburgh footfall data published by the City of Edinburgh Council.
It shows the foot traffic at 19 different locations over the course of a year in the City of Edinburgh.
